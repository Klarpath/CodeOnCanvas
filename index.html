Our Story: From European Roots to London Code

We are Code On Canvas, a family of developers united by a shared passion for clean code and exceptional design. Our story begins on the old continent, but our ambition led us to London, a city that beats with a digital pulse like no other. We built our business, Klarpath, to bring this vision to life.

Our journey was forged in the halls of London's most prestigious universities. We honed our craft securing Master's degrees with distinction from world-class IT programmes at campuses including Northumbria University London, Solent Southampton, and Roehampton University.

But our education wasn't just academic. We immersed ourselves in the vibrant London lifestyle, embracing the rich tapestry of British culture. We moved not just for opportunity, but to become part of the fabric of this cherished Island. Code On Canvas is the culmination of this journey—a blend of European precision, British creativity, and a deep understanding of what makes a digital presence truly powerful.
